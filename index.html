About Us

The Problem

Many university students have no idea what they will do after school. They have no skills and knowledge that can sustain them in the future. The traditional university does not in any way prepare them for the future of work.

The Solution

We propose Code4Campus, an initiative that helps university students to kickstart their tech journey while on campus. Code4Campus aims to provide career advice and introductory tech education to students. C4C is a student initiative - organised by students for students. Students who are skilled in a particular field serve as mentors and tutors to other students who want to break into that field.

Our Mission

Code4Campus's mission is to get one million university students to get their career started while on campus. We aim to do this by conducting both physical and online classes, creating and facilitating both physical and online communities of university students, offering career advice and providing them the tools and resources they need to grow.

Our Traction So far

1 Campus, 1 Bootcamp, 200+ members
